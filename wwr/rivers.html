Rivers To Do Rafting In Peru

Rafting on the Urubamba River

Be an adventurous traveler and enjoy the adrenaline rush of a rafting tour in Chuquicahuana. Navigate the Urubamba River with this full-day guided tour in Peru. Grab a helmet, wetsuit, and paddle to head out for a rafting lesson on the Level II and III rapids on this part of the river.

Rafting in the Cusco river

Embark on an exciting outdoor adventure that combines whitewater rafting that satisfies the need for speed. Tackle class III and IV rapids and battle intense waves as you make your way down the river. Depart Cusco for Oropesa, enjoying the extraordinary scenery of the surrounding Andes as you go.

Rafting in Lunahuana

A call to all adventure lovers, if you are into adventure sports you cannot miss this full-day excursion experience to go rafting in Lunahuaná is guaranteed to get your pulse racing. Depart from Lima and go whitewater rafting with the first stop at the Canete River, whose rapids range from relatively gentle class III to intense class V. Explore the area on a quad bike, visit Cerro Azul, and much more.
